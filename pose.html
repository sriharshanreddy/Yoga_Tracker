Yoga Pose Library

Browse and learn foundational yoga poses categorized by purpose.

Tadasana (Mountain Pose)

Improves posture and alignment. Great as a starting or resting pose.

Vrikshasana (Tree Pose)

Improves balance and strengthens legs and spine.

Phalakasana (Plank)

Builds core strength and endurance.

Balasana (Child’s Pose)

Gentle stretch for back and hips. Restful and calming.

Virabhadrasana (Warrior II)

Strengthens thighs, shoulders, and improves stamina.

Setu Bandhasana (Bridge Pose)

Stretches chest and spine. Stimulates digestion and calms the brain.
